General notes:

This mod does several things:

1. It decensores(?) the fascist civil flag.
2. It adds cosmetic tags for the war flags of Germany (as of now Uncle A and Kaiser Willy).
3. It uses two events to switch the fascist war flag at the appopriate date.
4. It uses several events to switch your flag, depending on your diplomatic status (war or peace).
